# Ciwon Suga

## Bayani

Ciwon Suga (Diabetes) cuta ce mai ɗorewa wadda ke faruwa idan jiki ba zai iya daidaita yawan sukari (glucose) da ke cikin jini yadda ya kamata ba.

Glucose shi ne babban tushen kuzari ga jiki. Insulin, wani sinadari da ƙodar pancreas ke samarwa, yana taimaka wa glucose shiga cikin ƙwayoyin jiki domin samar da kuzari. Idan insulin bai isa ba ko kuma jiki bai iya amfani da shi yadda ya kamata ba, yawan sukari a jini zai ƙaru.

Idan ba a kula da ciwon suga ba, yana iya haifar da matsaloli masu tsanani da suka shafi zuciya, ƙoda, idanu, jijiyoyi da sauran sassan jiki.

---

# Alamomin da Aka Fi Gani

Alamomin ciwon suga sun haɗa da:

- Yawan yin fitsari
- Yawan jin ƙishirwa
- Yawan jin yunwa
- Gajiya
- Rage nauyin jiki ba tare da wani dalili ba
- Ganin abubuwa ba a sarari

Wasu mutane na iya kasancewa da ciwon suga ba tare da sun lura da wata alama ba, musamman a farkon cutar.

---

# Abubuwan Hadari

Abubuwan da ke ƙara haɗarin kamuwa da ciwon suga sun haɗa da:

- Tarihin ciwon suga a cikin iyali
- Kiba ko yawan nauyin jiki
- Rashin motsa jiki
- Cin abinci mara kyau
- Tsufa ko ƙaruwa da shekaru

---

# Rigakafi

Ana iya rage haɗarin kamuwa da ciwon suga ta hanyar:

- Cin abinci mai lafiya da daidaito
- Yin motsa jiki akai-akai
- Kula da nauyin jiki
- Yin gwajin lafiya lokaci zuwa lokaci

Canjin salon rayuwa mai kyau na taimakawa wajen rage haɗarin kamuwa da ciwon suga, musamman Nau'i na 2.

---

# Magani

Maganin ciwon suga na iya haɗawa da:

- Cin abinci mai lafiya
- Yin motsa jiki akai-akai
- Auna yawan sukari a jini
- Shan magungunan rage sukari ta baki
- Yin allurar insulin

Ya kamata marasa lafiya su bi umarnin ƙwararren ma'aikacin lafiya kuma su ci gaba da zuwa duba lafiya akai-akai.

---

# Alamomin Gaggawa

A nemi kulawar gaggawa nan da nan idan mutum yana da:

- Rashin sani (Suma)
- Wahalar numfashi
- Tsananin rashin ruwa a jiki
- Rikicewar hankali

Waɗannan alamomi na iya nuna cewa ciwon suga ya tsananta kuma yana buƙatar kulawar gaggawa.

---

# Gargadin Likita

An tanadi wannan bayani ne domin ilimantarwa kawai.

Ba ya maye gurbin shawarar ƙwararren ma'aikacin lafiya, ganewar asali ko magani.

Kafin ka fara ko ka canza kowace irin magani, ka tuntubi ƙwararren ma'aikacin lafiya.
