# Kwayar Cutar Rage Garkuwar Jiki (HIV) da Cutar AIDS
Abubuwan da ke Haddasa HIV

HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus) cuta ce da kwayar HIV ke haifarwa. Wannan kwayar cuta tana kai hari ga garkuwar jikin mutum, musamman ƙwayoyin CD4 (T-helper cells). Idan ba a yi magani ba, HIV na raunana garkuwar jiki kuma zai iya kaiwa ga matakin AIDS.

Hanyoyin da HIV Ke Yaduwa

Ana kamuwa da HIV ta hanyar hulɗa da wasu ruwan jikin mutum mai ɗauke da cutar, kamar:

Jini
Maniyyi
Ruwan farji
Ruwan dubura
Nono (madarar uwa)

Mafi yawan hanyoyin kamuwa da HIV sun haɗa da:

1. Yin jima'i ba tare da kariya ba
Yin jima'in farji ko ta dubura ba tare da amfani da kwaroron roba ba da mutum mai ɗauke da HIV.
2. Raba allura ko sirinji
Amfani da allura ko sirinji da wani ya yi amfani da shi wajen yin allurar miyagun ƙwayoyi, zanen jiki (tattoo), ko huda jiki.
3. Daga uwa zuwa jariri
HIV na iya wucewa daga uwa mai ɗauke da cutar zuwa jaririnta lokacin ciki, haihuwa, ko shayarwa idan ba ta karɓi magani ba.
4. Karin jini mai ɗauke da HIV
Ana iya kamuwa idan an ƙara jinin da ba a tantance ba. Wannan yana da wuya a wuraren da ake gwada jini kafin ƙarin jini.
5. Kamuwa a wurin aiki
Ma'aikatan lafiya na iya kamuwa idan allurar da ta taɓa jinin mai HIV ta soke su ko kuma suka taɓa jinin da ya kamu.
Abubuwan da BA sa Yaɗa HIV

Ba a kamuwa da HIV ta hanyar:

Gaisawa ko runguma
Raba abinci ko abin sha
Cizon sauro ko sauran kwari
Tari ko atishawa
Amfani da bayan gida ɗaya
Yin iyo a tafki ko wurin wanka tare
Mu'amalar yau da kullum
Hanyoyin Kare Kai
Yi amfani da kwaroron roba yadda ya kamata a duk lokacin jima'i.
Kada a raba allura ko sirinji da wani.
A riƙa yin gwajin HIV akai-akai idan kana cikin haɗarin kamuwa.
A yi amfani da PrEP (Maganin kariya kafin kamuwa da HIV) ga masu haɗarin kamuwa.
A yi amfani da PEP (Maganin kariya bayan yiwuwar kamuwa da HIV) cikin sa'o'i 72 bayan yiwuwar kamuwa.
A tabbatar da cewa jinin da za a ƙara an tantance shi.
Mata masu juna biyu masu HIV su riƙa shan magungunan ART domin rage yiwuwar watsa cutar ga jariri.

## Bayani

**HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus)** kwayar cuta ce da ke kai hari ga garkuwar jikin mutum, musamman ƙwayoyin **CD4 (T-helper cells)** waɗanda ke taimakawa jiki wajen yaƙar cututtuka.

Idan ba a yi magani ba, HIV yana raunana garkuwar jiki a hankali, wanda hakan ke sa jiki ya kasa kare kansa daga cututtuka da wasu nau'ikan cutar daji.

**AIDS (Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome)** ita ce mafi tsanancin matakin kamuwa da HIV.

Ba duk wanda yake ɗauke da HIV ba ne zai kai matakin AIDS. Idan an gano cutar da wuri kuma aka fara **Maganin Rigakafin Ƙwayoyin Cuta (ART)** tare da ci gaba da shan maganin yadda ya kamata, mutane da yawa suna rayuwa lafiya, suna aiki, kuma suna rayuwa na dogon lokaci.

A yau, duk da cewa HIV har yanzu matsalar lafiya ce a duniya, magungunan zamani sun sa ana iya sarrafa cutar kamar sauran cututtuka masu ɗorewa.

# Bambanci Tsakanin HIV da AIDS

## HIV

- HIV ita ce ƙwayar cutar.
- Tana kai hari ga garkuwar jiki.
- Ana iya sarrafa ta da maganin ART na tsawon rayuwa.
- Mutum mai HIV na iya kasancewa cikin koshin lafiya na shekaru da dama.

---

## AIDS

- AIDS ita ce matakin ƙarshe na kamuwa da HIV.
- Yana faruwa idan garkuwar jiki ta yi matuƙar rauni.
- Mutanen da ke da AIDS suna cikin haɗarin kamuwa da cututtuka masu tsanani da wasu nau'ikan cutar daji.

Ba kowane mutum mai HIV ba ne zai kai matakin AIDS, musamman idan ya fara magani da wuri.

---

# Abubuwan da ke Haddasa Cutar

HIV na faruwa ne sakamakon kamuwa da **Kwayar Cutar Rage Garkuwar Jiki (Human Immunodeficiency Virus).**

Kwayar cutar tana kai hari ga ƙwayoyin **CD4** sannan tana lalata su a hankali, wanda hakan ke rage ikon jiki na yaƙar cututtuka.

---

# Hanyoyin Da HIV Ke Yaɗuwa

Ana iya kamuwa da HIV ta hanyoyi masu zuwa:

- Jima'i ba tare da kariya ba ta farji.
- Jima'i ta dubura ba tare da kariya ba.
- Amfani da allura ko sirinji guda da aka riga aka yi amfani da shi.
- Karɓar jinin da ke ɗauke da HIV (inda ba a tantance jinin ba).
- Daga uwa zuwa jariri lokacin ciki, haihuwa ko shayarwa.
- Hatsarin allura ga ma'aikatan lafiya.

Ruwan jiki da ke iya yaɗa HIV sun haɗa da:

- Jini
- Maniyyi
- Ruwan farji
- Ruwan dubura
- Nono

---

# HIV BA Ya Yaɗuwa Ta

HIV ba ya yaɗuwa ta hanyar:

- Gaisawa da hannu
- Runguma
- Raba abinci
- Raba kofin sha
- Amfani da bayan-gida guda
- Cizon sauro
- Gumi
- Hawaye
- Yawu yayin hulɗar yau da kullum
- Tari ko atishawa

Mutanen da ke rayuwa da HIV bai kamata a nuna musu wariya ba, domin cutar ba ta yaɗuwa ta hulɗar yau da kullum.

---

# Abubuwan Hadari

Haɗarin kamuwa da HIV ya fi yawa ga mutanen da:

- Ke yin jima'i ba tare da kariya ba.
- Ke da abokan jima'i da yawa.
- Ke amfani da allura tare da wasu.
- Ke da wasu cututtukan da ake ɗauka ta hanyar jima'i (STIs).
- Suka karɓi jinin da ba a tantance ba.
- Ke fuskantar haɗarin hulɗa da jinin masu cutar saboda aikinsu.

---

# Matakan Kamuwa da HIV

## Mataki na 1: Sabuwar Kamuwa da HIV (Acute HIV Infection)

Wannan mataki yana faruwa cikin makonni bayan kamuwa da cutar.

Alamomi na iya haɗawa da:

- Zazzabi
- Kuraje
- Ciwon makogwaro
- Kumburin ƙwayoyin lymph
- Ciwon tsoka
- Ciwon kai
- Gajiya
- Gyambon baki

Wasu mutane ba sa nuna wata alama a wannan mataki.

---

## Mataki na 2: HIV Mai Tsawon Lokaci (Chronic HIV Infection)

Wannan mataki na iya ɗaukar shekaru masu yawa.

- Kwayar cutar tana ci gaba da kasancewa a jiki.
- Sau da yawa ba ta nuna alamomi masu yawa.
- Idan ba a yi magani ba, tana ci gaba da lalata garkuwar jiki.

---

## Mataki na 3: AIDS

Wannan shi ne mafi tsananin matakin kamuwa da HIV.

A wannan mataki:

- Garkuwar jiki ta yi rauni sosai.
- Jiki yana cikin haɗarin kamuwa da cututtukan da ke anfani da raunin garkuwar jiki da kuma wasu nau'ikan cutar daji.

---

# Alamomin HIV

Alamomin HIV suna bambanta gwargwadon matakin da cutar take.

Alamomin da za su iya bayyana sun haɗa da:

- Zazzabi
- Gajiya
- Kumburin ƙwayoyin lymph
- Zufar dare
- Rage nauyin jiki
- Gudawa mai ɗorewa
- Tari mai ɗorewa
- Gyambon baki
- Kurajen fata
- Yawan kamuwa da cututtuka

Wasu mutane ba sa nuna wata alama tsawon shekaru, musamman idan suna shan maganin ART yadda ya kamata.

---

# Alamomin HIV/AIDS Mai Tsanani

A je a duba lafiya idan mutum yana da:

- Rage nauyin jiki sosai ba tare da wani dalili ba
- Zazzabi mai ɗorewa
- Zufar dare akai-akai
- Gudawa mai tsanani ko mai ɗorewa
- Tari mai ɗorewa
- Wahalar numfashi
- Yawan kamuwa da ciwon huhu (Pneumonia)
- Tarin fuka (TB)
- Farin gyambo a baki (Oral Thrush)
- Tsananin rauni
- Rikicewar hankali
- Farfaɗiya

Waɗannan alamomi na iya nuna cewa garkuwar jiki ta yi rauni sosai kuma suna buƙatar kulawar gaggawa.

---

# Cututtukan da Ke anfani da Raunin Garkuwar Jiki

Mutanen da HIV ya raunana garkuwar jikinsu na iya kamuwa da:

- Tarin Fuka (TB)
- Ciwon Huhu (Pneumonia)
- Farin gyambo a baki (Oral Candidiasis)
- Cryptococcal Meningitis
- Toxoplasmosis
- Cytomegalovirus (CMV)
- Gudawa mai ɗorewa
- Kaposi Sarcoma
- Cutar dajin mahaifar mata (Cervical Cancer)
- Sauran cututtuka masu tsanani

Fara maganin **ART** da wuri yana rage haɗarin kamuwa da waɗannan cututtuka.

---

# Ganowa

Ma'aikatan lafiya na iya gano HIV ta hanyar:

- Gwajin HIV na gaggawa (Rapid HIV Test)
- Gwajin ƙwayoyin kariya (HIV Antibody Test)
- Gwajin Antigen/Antibody
- Gwajin HIV PCR (musamman ga jarirai)
- Gwajin Viral Load
- Auna yawan ƙwayoyin CD4

Idan gwajin farko ya nuna mutum na da HIV, ana tabbatar da sakamakon bisa ƙa'idodin gwajin ƙasa.

---

# Magani

A halin yanzu **babu maganin da ke kawar da HIV gaba ɗaya.**

Sai dai ana iya sarrafa cutar yadda ya kamata ta amfani da **Maganin Rigakafin Ƙwayoyin Cuta (Antiretroviral Therapy – ART).**

### Manufofin ART

- Rage yawan ƙwayoyin cutar (Viral Load)
- Kare garkuwar jiki
- Hana cutar kaiwa matakin AIDS
- Inganta rayuwar marar lafiya
- Rage yiwuwar yaɗa HIV ga wasu

Ya kamata a fara ART da zarar an gano HIV.

Marasa lafiya su:

- Sha magani kowace rana.
- Kada su tsallake shan magani.
- Ci gaba da zuwa asibiti don dubawa.
- Rika duba Viral Load da CD4 kamar yadda likita ya ba da shawara.

Bin tsarin magani yadda ya kamata yana da matuƙar muhimmanci domin samun sakamako mai kyau.

---

# Rayuwa Lafiya da HIV

Mutanen da ke rayuwa da HIV za su iya rayuwa cikin ƙoshin lafiya idan suna:

- Shan ART kullum kamar yadda aka rubuta.
- Cin abinci mai gina jiki.
- Yin motsa jiki akai-akai.
- Samun isasshen barci.
- Gujewa shan taba sigari.
- Rage ko gujewa shan barasa.
- Rage damuwa.
- Halartar dukkan alƙawuran asibiti.
- Yin alluran rigakafin da aka ba da shawara.

Kulawa da lafiyar kwakwalwa da samun goyon bayan iyali da al'umma suna da muhimmanci ga rayuwa mai kyau.

---

# Rigakafi

Ana iya rage haɗarin kamuwa da HIV ta hanyoyi masu zuwa:

- Yin amfani da kwaroron roba yadda ya kamata a duk lokacin saduwa.
- Yin gwajin HIV akai-akai.
- Kasancewa da abokin aure ko abokin jima'i guda ɗaya wanda ba ya da HIV.
- Gujewa amfani da allura ko sirinji tare da wasu.
- Tabbatar da cewa jinin da za a ƙara wa mutum an gwada shi kuma ba ya ɗauke da HIV.
- Yin kaciyar maza ta hanyar lafiya inda ya dace.
- Hana yaɗuwar HIV daga uwa zuwa jariri (**PMTCT**).
- Amfani da **PrEP (Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis)** ga mutanen da ke cikin haɗarin kamuwa da HIV.
- Amfani da **PEP (Post-Exposure Prophylaxis)** cikin sa'o'i **72** bayan yiwuwar kamuwa da HIV.
- Fara maganin **ART** da wuri ga mutanen da ke rayuwa da HIV.

Mutanen da suke shan ART yadda ya kamata kuma **viral load** ɗinsu ya zama ba a iya ganowa (*Undetectable*) suna rage yiwuwar yaɗa HIV ga abokan jima'insu sosai (**U=U: Undetectable = Untransmittable**).

---

# HIV Lokacin Juna Biyu

Mace mai juna biyu da ke rayuwa da HIV na iya rage yiwuwar yaɗa HIV ga jaririnta ta hanyar:

- Fara shan ART da wuri.
- Halartar kulawar masu juna biyu (Antenatal Care).
- Bin shawarar ma'aikatan lafiya yayin haihuwa.
- Tabbatar da cewa jariri ya samu kulawar da ta dace bayan haihuwa.

Da magani mai kyau, mata da yawa masu HIV suna haifan jarirai **marasa HIV**.

---

# Matsalolin da Ka Iya Taso

Idan ba a yi magani ba, HIV na iya haifar da:

- AIDS
- Tarin Fuka (TB)
- Ciwon Huhu (Pneumonia)
- Yawan kamuwa da cututtuka
- Tsananin rage nauyin jiki
- Matsalolin jijiyoyin kwakwalwa
- Wasu nau'ikan cutar daji
- Ciwon koda
- Ciwon hanta
- Mutuwa

Fara ART da wuri yana rage yiwuwar waɗannan matsaloli.

---

# Lokacin Neman Agajin Gaggawa

A je asibiti nan da nan idan mutum mai HIV yana da:

- Wahalar numfashi
- Zazzabi mai tsanani da ba ya sauka
- Matsanancin ciwon ƙirji
- Rikicewar hankali
- Farfaɗiya
- Tsananin rashin ruwa a jiki
- Suma
- Amai mai ci gaba
- Tsananin rauni
- Alamomin sankarau (Meningitis)

Waɗannan alamomi na iya nuna cewa akwai wata cuta mai tsanani da ke buƙatar kulawar gaggawa.

---

# Tambayoyin da Aka Fi Yi

## Shin akwai maganin da ke warkar da HIV gaba ɗaya?

A'a.

A halin yanzu babu maganin da ke kawar da HIV gaba ɗaya. Amma maganin **ART** yana ba mutane da yawa damar rayuwa lafiya na tsawon lokaci.

---

## Ana iya rigakafin HIV?

Eh.

Amfani da kwaroron roba, yin gwajin HIV, amfani da **PrEP** da **PEP**, gujewa amfani da allura tare da wasu, amfani da jinin da aka gwada, da fara ART da wuri suna taimakawa wajen rage haɗarin kamuwa da HIV.

---

## Mutum mai HIV na iya samun yara?

Eh.

Da kulawar lafiya mai kyau da kuma ART, mutane da yawa masu HIV suna samun ciki lafiya kuma suna haifan jarirai marasa HIV.

---

## Sauro na iya yaɗa HIV?

A'a.

Sauro da sauran kwari ba sa yaɗa HIV.

---

## Ana iya kamuwa da HIV ta hanyar sumbata?

A'a.

Sumbatar yau da kullum ba ta yaɗa HIV. HIV ba ya yaɗuwa ta yawu yayin hulɗar yau da kullum.

---

## Me ya sa shan ART kullum yake da muhimmanci?

Shan ART kowace rana yana:

- Rage yawan ƙwayoyin cutar (Viral Load)
- Kare garkuwar jiki
- Inganta lafiyar mutum
- Rage yiwuwar yaɗa HIV ga wasu

---

# Muhimman Bayanai

- HIV yana kai hari ga garkuwar jiki.
- AIDS ita ce mafi tsananin matakin HIV idan ba a yi magani ba.
- HIV yana yaɗuwa ne ta wasu ruwan jiki kawai, ba ta hulɗar yau da kullum ba.
- Gwajin HIV da fara magani da wuri suna inganta lafiya.
- ART yana ba mutane da yawa damar rayuwa lafiya na dogon lokaci.
- Rigakafin HIV ya haɗa da amfani da kwaroron roba, PrEP, PEP, gujewa amfani da allura tare da wasu, da amfani da jinin da aka gwada.
- Mata masu juna biyu da ke rayuwa da HIV na iya rage yiwuwar yaɗa HIV ga jariransu ta hanyar kulawar lafiya da ART.

---

# Gargadin Likita

An tanadi wannan bayani ne domin ilimantarwa kawai.

Ba ya maye gurbin shawarar ƙwararren ma'aikacin lafiya, ganewar asali, ko magani.

Idan kana tunanin ka kamu da HIV, ko kuma ka yi hulɗar da za ta iya sa ka kamu da cutar, je cibiyar lafiya mafi kusa domin yin gwaji da samun shawara cikin gaggawa. Idan ya dace, a fara **PEP cikin sa'o'i 72** bayan yiwuwar kamuwa.

Idan kana rayuwa da HIV, ka ci gaba da shan **ART** kamar yadda aka rubuta kuma ka rika zuwa dukkan alƙawuran asibiti.

Koyaushe bi shawarar ƙwararrun ma'aikatan lafiya.
