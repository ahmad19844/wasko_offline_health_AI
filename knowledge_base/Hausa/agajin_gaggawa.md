# Agajin Gaggawa na Farko

## Bayani

Agajin gaggawa na farko shi ne taimakon farko da ake ba mutum da ya ji rauni ko ya kamu da rashin lafiya kwatsam kafin ƙwararrun ma'aikatan lafiya su iso.

Babban manufar agajin farko ita ce:

- Ceton rai
- Hana yanayin ya ƙara tsananta
- Taimakawa wajen murmurewa
- Kare lafiyar wanda ya ji rauni har sai an kai shi asibiti

Agajin farko ba ya maye gurbin cikakken magani daga ƙwararrun ma'aikatan lafiya.

---

# Manufofin Agajin Farko

Babban manufofin agajin farko sun haɗa da:

- Ceton rai
- Hana ƙarin rauni
- Taimakawa wajen murmurewa
- Rage ciwo idan zai yiwu
- Kwantar wa wanda ya ji rauni hankali
- Neman taimakon likita cikin gaggawa

---

# Tsarin DRSABCD

DRSABCD hanya ce da ake bi yayin ba da agajin gaggawa.

## D – Danger (Haɗari)

Tabbatar wurin yana da aminci gare ka, wanda ya ji rauni da sauran mutane kafin ka kusance shi.

Kada ka jefa kanka cikin haɗari.

---

## R – Response (Martani)

Ka yi magana da wanda ya ji rauni sannan ka taɓa kafadarsa a hankali domin ganin ko yana amsawa.

Idan bai amsa ba, nemi taimako nan take.

---

## S – Send for Help (Neman Taimako)

Kira lambar gaggawa ta yankinku ko ka nemi wani ya kira.

Ka bayar da:

- Wurin da abin ya faru
- Irin matsalar
- Adadin mutanen da suka ji rauni
- Lambar wayarka

---

## A – Airway (Hanyar Numfashi)

Tabbatar hanyar numfashi a buɗe take.

Idan babu zargin raunin wuya, a buɗe hanyar numfashi a hankali.

Ka cire abin da yake toshe numfashi idan kana ganinsa.

Kada ka sanya yatsa cikin baki ba tare da gani ba.

---

## B – Breathing (Numfashi)

Duba ko mutum yana numfashi yadda ya kamata.

Idan baya numfashi kuma kana da horo, fara CPR idan hakan ba zai jefa ka cikin haɗari ba.

---

## C – Circulation (Yawo da Jini)

Duba ko akwai zubar jini mai tsanani.

Dakatar da zubar jini ta hanyar matsawa wurin da hannu ko bandeji mai tsafta.

---

## D – Defibrillation (AED)

Idan akwai na'urar AED, bi umarninta yayin da ake ci gaba da CPR har sai jami'an lafiya sun iso.

---

# Kare Kanka

Kafin ka ba da agajin farko:

- Tabbatar wurin yana da aminci
- Sanya safar hannu idan akwai
- Guji hulɗa kai tsaye da jini ko ruwan jiki
- Wanke hannuwa bayan kammala agajin
- Zubar da kayan da suka gurɓata yadda ya dace

---

# Kayan Agajin Farko

Akwatin agajin farko na iya ƙunsar:

- Filastar rufe ƙanana raunuka
- Gauze mai tsafta
- Medical tape
- Bandejin roba
- Maganin kashe ƙwayoyin cuta
- Safar hannu
- Almakashi
- Tweezers
- Ma'aunin zafin jiki
- Cold pack
- Oral Rehydration Solution (ORS)
- Kariya ta CPR
- Fitila
- Jerin lambobin gaggawa

A duba kayan lokaci-lokaci sannan a maye gurbin waɗanda suka lalace ko suka ƙare.

---

# Kula da Rauni

## Matakai

- Wanke hannuwa
- Dakatar da zubar jini
- Wanke raunin da ruwa mai tsafta
- Cire datti idan ana gani
- Shafa maganin kashe ƙwayoyin cuta idan ya dace
- Rufe da bandeji mai tsafta
- Sauya bandeji akai-akai

Nemi likita idan raunin ya yi zurfi ko ya kamu da ƙwayoyin cuta.

---

# Dakatar da Zubar Jini

- Matsa wurin da ƙarfi
- Yi amfani da kyalle ko bandeji mai tsafta
- Ci gaba da matsawa har sai jini ya tsaya
- Ɗaga hannun ko ƙafar idan ya dace

Nemi taimakon gaggawa idan zubar jinin ya yi yawa.

Kada a cire abin da ya soka cikin jiki.

---

# Zubar Jini daga Hanci

- Zauna ka jingina gaba
- Matse hancin na tsawon minti 10 zuwa 15
- Yi numfashi ta baki
- Kada ka busa hanci nan take

Nemi taimakon likita idan bai tsaya ba.

---

# Ƙananan Ƙonewa

- Zuba ruwan sanyi a wurin na minti 20
- Cire zobba ko kayan ado idan ba su manne ba
- Rufe da bandeji mara manne

Kada:

- Ka shafa mai ko man shanu
- Ka fashe kumfa

---

# Karaya

## Alamomi

- Matsanancin ciwo
- Kumburi
- Nakasa
- Rashin iya motsi

## Agajin Farko

- Hana motsa wurin
- Sanya cold pack
- Kai asibiti

Kada ka gyara ƙashin da ya karye.

---

# Murɗewar Tsoka (RICE)

- Huta
- Sanya kankara
- Matse da bandeji
- Ɗaga wurin sama

---

# Gwauron Gaba

- Hana motsa haɗin gwiwa
- Sanya kankara
- Kai asibiti

Kada ka mayar da ƙashi wurinsa.

---

# Shakewa

Idan mutum yana tari yadda ya kamata, ƙarfafa shi ya ci gaba da tari.

Idan baya iya magana ko numfashi:

- Kira lambar gaggawa nan take
- Ba shi taimakon farko idan kana da horo

Idan ya suma:

- Fara CPR idan kana da horo.

---

# CPR

Ana amfani da CPR idan mutum ya daina numfashi ko zuciyarsa ta tsaya.

- Kira lambar gaggawa
- Fara matsa kirji
- Yi amfani da AED idan akwai
- Ci gaba har sai taimako ya iso

---

# Matsayin Farfaɗowa

Idan mutum baya magana amma yana numfashi:

- Kwantar da shi a gefe
- Buɗe hanyar numfashi
- Ci gaba da sa ido

---

# Guba

- Cire mutum daga wurin gubar cikin aminci
- Kira cibiyar kula da guba ko lambar gaggawa
- Riƙe kwalin maganin idan zai yiwu

Kada ka sa mutum ya yi amai sai idan ƙwararren lafiya ya umarta.

---

# Cizon Dabba

- Wanke rauni da sabulu da ruwa na minti 15
- Rufe da bandeji mai tsafta
- Kai asibiti

Ana iya buƙatar allurar tetanus ko rabies.

---

# Cizon Maciji

- Kwantar da hankalin mutum
- Hana motsa ƙafar ko hannun da aka cije
- Cire zobe ko abin da ya matse

Kai asibiti cikin gaggawa.

Kada ka yanke ko tsotse wurin cizon.

---

# Matsanancin Zafi

## Gajiyar Zafi

- Kai mutum wuri mai sanyi
- Ba shi ruwa idan yana cikin hayyacinsa
- Rage zafin jikinsa

## Heat Stroke (Gaggawa)

- Kira lambar gaggawa
- Fara sanyaya jiki nan take

---

# Sumewa

- Kwantar da mutum
- Ɗaga ƙafafunsa
- Sassauta tufafi
- Ci gaba da lura da numfashi

---

# Farfaɗiya

- Kare mutum daga rauni
- Kada ka riƙe shi da ƙarfi
- Kada ka saka wani abu cikin bakinsa
- Lura da tsawon lokacin farfaɗiyar

Nemi taimakon likita idan ta wuce minti 5.

---

# Raunin Ido

- Kada ka goge ido
- Wanke da ruwa mai tsafta idan ya dace
- Rufe ido a hankali
- Kai asibiti

Kada ka cire abin da ya soka cikin ido.

---

# Girgizar Wutar Lantarki

- Kashe wutar kafin ka taɓa mutum
- Kira lambar gaggawa
- Duba numfashi
- Fara CPR idan ya zama dole
