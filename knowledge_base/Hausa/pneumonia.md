# Ciwon Huhu (Pneumonia)

## Bayani

Ciwon Huhu (Pneumonia) cuta ce da ke kama huhu, inda ƙananan jakunkunan iska na huhu (alveoli) suke cika da ruwa ko majina mai ɗauke da ƙwayoyin cuta. Wannan yana rage ikon huhu na isar da iskar oxygen zuwa jiki, wanda hakan kan sa numfashi ya zama da wahala.

Ciwon huhu na daga cikin manyan cututtukan da ke haddasa rashin lafiya da mutuwa a duniya, musamman ga ƙananan yara, tsofaffi, da mutanen da garkuwar jikinsu ta yi rauni.

Ana iya kamuwa da cutar ta hanyar:

- Ƙwayoyin cuta (Bacteria)
- Ƙwayoyin Virus
- Fungus
- Ko kuma idan abinci, ruwa ko amai ya shiga huhu (Aspiration)

Gano cutar da wuri da fara magani cikin lokaci yana ƙara damar samun cikakkiyar lafiya.

---

# Abubuwan da ke Haddasa Cutar

Ciwon huhu na iya kasancewa sakamakon nau'o'in ƙwayoyin cuta daban-daban.

## Ƙwayoyin Bacteria

Waɗanda aka fi samu sun haɗa da:

- *Streptococcus pneumoniae* (wanda aka fi samu)
- *Haemophilus influenzae*
- *Staphylococcus aureus*
- *Klebsiella pneumoniae*
- *Mycoplasma pneumoniae*

---

## Ƙwayoyin Virus

Sun haɗa da:

- Virus na mura (Influenza)
- Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV)
- Coronavirus (ciki har da COVID-19)
- Adenovirus

---

## Fungus

Ciwon huhu da fungus ke haddasawa yafi faruwa ga mutanen da garkuwar jikinsu ta yi rauni.

Misalai sun haɗa da:

- *Pneumocystis jirovecii*
- *Histoplasma*
- *Cryptococcus*

---

## Aspiration Pneumonia

Wannan nau'in yana faruwa ne idan:

- Abinci
- Ruwa
- Yawu
- Ko amai

sun shiga cikin huhu maimakon su wuce ta hanyar narkewar abinci.

---

# Abubuwan Hadari

Mutanen da ke cikin haɗarin kamuwa da ciwon huhu sun haɗa da:

- Yara masu ƙasa da shekara biyar
- Mutane masu shekara 65 ko sama da haka
- Masu shan taba sigari
- Masu ɗauke da HIV/AIDS
- Masu ciwon suga
- Masu cututtukan huhu na dogon lokaci
- Masu asma
- Masu cututtukan zuciya
- Masu rashin abinci mai gina jiki
- Masu cutar daji
- Marasa lafiya da ke kwance a asibiti
- Mutanen da garkuwar jikinsu ta yi rauni

---

# Nau'ikan Ciwon Huhu

## 1. Community-Acquired Pneumonia (CAP)

Wannan shi ne ciwon huhu da mutum ke kamuwa da shi a wajen asibiti ko cibiyar lafiya.

---

## 2. Hospital-Acquired Pneumonia (HAP)

Yana faruwa bayan mutum ya kwashe **awanni 48 ko fiye** yana kwance a asibiti.

---

## 3. Ventilator-Associated Pneumonia (VAP)

Wannan nau'in yana faruwa ga marasa lafiya da ke amfani da na'urar taimakon numfashi (Ventilator).

---

## 4. Aspiration Pneumonia

Yana faruwa idan abinci, ruwa, amai ko abin da ke cikin ciki ya shiga cikin huhu.

---

# Alamomi da Cutar Ke Nunawa

Alamomin ciwon huhu na iya bayyana cikin gaggawa ko kuma a hankali.

Alamomin da aka fi gani sun haɗa da:

- Tari mai ɗorewa
- Tari mai fitar da majina
- Zazzabi
- Jin sanyi tare da rawar jiki
- Wahalar numfashi
- Ciwon ƙirji wanda ke ƙaruwa yayin numfashi ko tari
- Numfashi da sauri
- Gajiya
- Rauni
- Zufa mai yawa
- Rashin sha'awar abinci
- Ciwon kai
- Ciwon tsoka

### Alamomin da yara kan nuna

Yara na iya samun:

- Numfashi da sauri
- Rashin shan nono ko cin abinci
- Yawan kuka ko rashin natsuwa
- Kara mai ƙarfi yayin numfashi (grunting)
- Leɓɓa ko fata mai shuɗi idan cutar ta yi tsanani

### Alamomin da tsofaffi kan nuna

Tsofaffi na iya samun:

- Rikicewar hankali
- Rage farkawa
- Tsananin rauni
- Rashin sha'awar abinci

---

# Alamomin Cuta Mai Tsanani

A nemi kulawar gaggawa nan da nan idan marar lafiya yana da:

- Matsananciyar wahalar numfashi
- Shuɗewar leɓɓa ko farce
- Matsanancin ciwon ƙirji
- Rikicewar hankali
- Zazzabi mai tsanani da ba ya sauka
- Ƙarancin iskar oxygen a jiki idan an auna
- Alamomin da ke ƙara muni cikin sauri
- Rashin iya shan ruwa
- Amai mai ci gaba
- Suma

Waɗannan alamomi na buƙatar kulawar gaggawa a asibiti.

---

# Matsalolin da Ka Iya Taso

Idan ba a yi magani ba, ciwon huhu na iya haifar da:

- Gazawar numfashi
- Yaɗuwar ƙwayar cuta cikin jini (Sepsis)
- Taruwar ƙurji a cikin huhu (Lung Abscess)
- Taruwar ruwa a gefen huhu (Pleural Effusion)
- Taruwar mugunya a gefen huhu (Empyema)
- Matsanancin gazawar numfashi (ARDS)
- Gazawar koda
- Matsalolin zuciya
- Mutuwa

Magani cikin lokaci yana rage haɗarin waɗannan matsaloli.

---

# Ganowa

Ma'aikatan lafiya na iya gano ciwon huhu ta hanyar:

- Tarihin rashin lafiya
- Duban marar lafiya
- Hoton X-ray na ƙirji
- Gwajin jini
- Auna yawan iskar oxygen a jini (Pulse Oximetry)
- Gwajin majina
- Gwajin al'adar jini (Blood Culture)
- CT Scan idan ya zama dole
- Gwajin ƙwayoyin Virus lokacin barkewar cuta

---

# Magani

Magani yana danganta da abin da ya haddasa cutar da kuma tsananinta.

## Idan ƙwayoyin bacteria ne suka haddasa cutar

Ana iya ba da maganin kashe ƙwayoyin cuta (Antibiotics) kamar yadda likita ya rubuta.

---

## Idan ƙwayoyin virus ne suka haddasa cutar

Magani na iya haɗawa da:

- Hutu
- Shan ruwa mai yawa
- Rage zazzabi
- Magungunan ƙwayoyin virus (Antiviral) idan ya dace

---

## Idan fungus ne ya haddasa cutar

Ana amfani da magungunan kashe fungus (Antifungal medicines) waɗanda ƙwararren ma'aikacin lafiya ya rubuta.

---

## Sauran Magunguna Masu Taimako

Dangane da tsananin cutar, ana iya buƙatar:

- Iskar oxygen
- Ruwan allura (IV Fluids)
- Magungunan rage zazzabi
- Kwantar da marar lafiya a asibiti

Ya kamata marar lafiya ya kammala dukkan magungunan da aka rubuta, ko da ya fara jin sauƙi.

---

# Kulawa a Gida

Idan marar lafiya yana murmurewa a gida, ya kamata ya:

- Huta sosai.
- Sha ruwa mai tsafta da yawa.
- Sha dukkan magungunan da aka rubuta yadda ya kamata.
- Ci abinci mai gina jiki.
- Guji shan taba sigari.
- Guji shan giya.
- Kula da alamomin cutar.
- Koma asibiti idan alamomin suka tsananta.

**Kada a daina shan maganin kashe ƙwayoyin cuta (Antibiotics) kafin a kammala lokacin da likita ya tsara, sai idan ƙwararren ma'aikacin lafiya ya ba da umarni.**

---

# Rigakafi

Ana iya rage haɗarin kamuwa da ciwon huhu ta hanyar:

- Yin alluran rigakafi da aka ba da shawara.
- Wanke hannu akai-akai da sabulu da ruwa.
- Gujewa shan taba sigari.
- Cin abinci mai gina jiki.
- Rufe baki da hanci lokacin tari ko atishawa.
- Sanya abin rufe fuska idan ya dace.
- Kula da cututtuka masu ɗorewa kamar ciwon suga da asma.
- Shayar da jarirai nono.
- Gujewa kusantar masu fama da cututtukan numfashi.

### Alluran rigakafin da ke taimakawa sun haɗa da:

- Allurar rigakafin **Pneumococcal**
- Allurar rigakafin **Influenza (Mura)**
- Allurar rigakafin **COVID-19** (inda ya dace)
- Allurar rigakafin **Haemophilus influenzae type b (Hib)**

---

# Abinci Lokacin Murmurewa

Cin abinci mai kyau yana taimakawa wajen saurin warkewa.

Ana ba da shawarar:

- 'Ya'yan itatuwa
- Kayan lambu
- Hatsi cikakke
- Wake
- Ƙwai
- Kifi
- Nama mara kitse
- Madara da sauran kayan madara
- Shan ruwa mai tsafta da yawa

Ga mutanen da ba sa jin yunwa sosai, cin ƙananan abinci sau da yawa na iya taimakawa.

---

# Lokacin Neman Agajin Gaggawa

Je asibiti mafi kusa nan da nan idan marar lafiya yana da:

- Matsananciyar wahalar numfashi
- Ciwon ƙirji mai tsanani
- Shuɗewar leɓɓa ko yatsun hannu
- Rikicewar hankali
- Zazzabi mai tsanani da ba ya sauka
- Tsananin rauni
- Iskar oxygen ƙasa da **90%** idan an auna
- Amai mai ci gaba
- Wahalar farkawa
- Suma

### Ga yara

Yaro mai:

- Numfashi da sauri,
- Wahalar shan nono ko cin abinci,
- Ko wahalar numfashi,

ya kamata a kai shi asibiti nan da nan.

---

# Tambayoyin da Aka Fi Yi

## Shin ciwon huhu yana yaɗuwa?

Wasu nau'ikan ciwon huhu da ƙwayoyin **bacteria** ko **virus** ke haifarwa na iya yaɗuwa daga mutum zuwa mutum ta hanyar ƙananan ɗigon numfashi da ke fita lokacin tari ko atishawa.

Kula da tsaftar jiki na rage yaduwar cutar.

---

## Ana iya warkar da ciwon huhu?

Eh.

Yawancin marasa lafiya suna warkewa gaba ɗaya idan an gano cutar da wuri kuma aka ba su maganin da ya dace.

---

## Shin ciwon huhu iri ɗaya ne da mura?

A'a.

Mura tana shafar hanyoyin numfashi na sama, yayin da ciwon huhu ke shafar huhu kai tsaye kuma yawanci ya fi tsanani.

---

## Su wa ne suka fi cikin haɗari?

Waɗanda suka fi cikin haɗari sun haɗa da:

- Ƙananan yara
- Tsofaffi
- Mata masu juna biyu
- Masu cututtuka masu ɗorewa
- Mutanen da garkuwar jikinsu ta yi rauni

---

## Ana iya rigakafin ciwon huhu?

Eh.

Alluran rigakafi, wanke hannu, cin abinci mai gina jiki, gujewa shan taba, da magance cututtukan numfashi da wuri suna taimakawa wajen rage haɗarin kamuwa da cutar.

---

# Muhimman Bayanai

- Ciwon huhu cuta ce da ke kama huhu.
- Ana iya haifar da ita ta ƙwayoyin **bacteria**, **virus**, **fungus**, ko kuma idan abinci ko ruwa ya shiga huhu.
- Alamomin da aka fi gani sun haɗa da tari, zazzabi, ciwon ƙirji, da wahalar numfashi.
- Ƙananan yara da tsofaffi sun fi cikin haɗarin kamuwa da cuta mai tsanani.
- Gano cutar da wuri da fara magani cikin lokaci yana ƙara damar warkewa.
- Alluran rigakafi da tsaftar jiki suna taimakawa wajen hana ciwon huhu.

---

# Gargadin Likita

An tanadi wannan bayani ne domin ilimantarwa kawai.

Ba ya maye gurbin shawarar ƙwararren ma'aikacin lafiya, ganewar asali, ko magani.

Idan kana fama da matsananciyar wahalar numfashi, ciwon ƙirji mai tsanani, shuɗewar leɓɓa, rikicewar hankali, zazzabi mai ɗorewa, ko alamomin da ke ƙara muni, je cibiyar lafiya mafi kusa nan da nan.

Koyaushe bi shawarar ƙwararrun ma'aikatan lafiya kuma ka kammala dukkan magungunan da aka rubuta.
